Fun little Napalm Recipies:

1) 1 part Motor Oil
   1 part soap chips or liquid soap
   2 parts gasoline

   Mix these products, and you have a basic napalm. Nothing fancy. The oil
   helps it stick, along with the soap.

2) 1 cup gasoline
   8 styrofoam packing peanuts

   Use this ratio as nessesary to make as much as you need. Allow the peanuts
   to dissolve in the gas. This makes a very thick, viscous napalm. You may
   alter the ratio as you wish. This is just a starting point.

3) 1 part Desiel Fuel
   
   Just put this in a container, and stick it in the freezer until it becomes
   slushy like a squishy. Then light and drop off a roof or something.

Those are the three best methods I know of. Enjoy!
