Ericsson Carfone IV DX Series

Manufactured by GE, dual NAM.

To Program:

1 > Power On

2 > 04049 [FCN] [FCN]

3 > Phone will display ESN

[SEND] steps, [END] saves and exits.

Steps:

  • 1 Area Code
  • 2 Mobile Number
  • 3 System ID
  • 4 Local Use
  • 5 MIN Option
  • 6 Initial Paging Channel
  • 7 Access Overload Class
  • 8 Preferred system
  • 9 Group ID
  • 10 4 digit security code
  • 11 handsfree, 1=enable
  • 12 hord alert
  • 13 autonomous registration
  • 14 Whereabouts known
  • 15 audible call timer
  • 16 continuous dtmf
  • 17 dual NAM
