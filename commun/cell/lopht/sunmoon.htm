SUN MOON STAR RADIENT 950MX

NOTES:  This is a quad NAM unit.
        The ESN prefix is 178 decimal & B2 hax.
        Sun Moon Star: 408-452-1119

NAM PROGRAMING:

1.  Turn power on.

2.  Enter FCN FCN * 9 5 0 6 2 6 *.

3.  Phone will display the software version, press up arrow.

4.  Phone will display NAM number, within five seconds press an alternate
    NAM if required.  Press up arrow.

5.  The # key stores each step.

6.  The up arrow key increments the step number.

7.  At any time press FCN END to complete and exit programing.

PROGRAMING DATA:

02       CAN NOT BE CHANGED  SCM 8          STATION CLASS MARK
