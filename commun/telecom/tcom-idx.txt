 issue 300). See other indexes in the Archives for 1989-1994 issues.
 It is gradually being expanded to include digests prior to 
 volume 9, but the technical problems are pervasive, to say the
 least. Reliability is NOT guarenteed prior to April, 1989, or
 about issue 400 of volume 9, due to a change in the way the
 Digest was produced. 

 After finding the desired subjects or authors, you would note 
 the archives file location (the numbers on the left are the volume 
 and issue numbers, packaged in groups of fifty issues).
