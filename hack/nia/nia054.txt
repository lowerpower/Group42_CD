Okay, here's an interesting file just to have laying around, this is a
list of the city codes in the soviet union.

   If an asterisk appears immediately after city name it means that,
for 5 or 6 digit numbers you should add '2' or '22', respectively,
before the number
           i.e. 12345 -> 2212345 or 123456 -> 2123456.
 
If there is NO asterisk you should put '0' or '00'
           i.e. 12345 -> 0012345 or 123456 -> 0123456
 
   You should ALWAYS dial '0' if it appears in the area code!
 
   The area code for MOSCOW is 095.
