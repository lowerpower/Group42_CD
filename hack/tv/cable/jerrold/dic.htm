Jerrold DIC series Full Service Modification

by 4x of Group 42

Instructions

    Older Models

      Older models, 36 channel, 3x12 switch matrix versions, have a resistor matrix consisting of 36 680ohm resistors. Removing the resistor appropriate to the channel desired, or punching out the hole in the track leading to the resistor decodes the channel.

    Newer Models

      Nower models have an addressing module accessable from the bottom. Shorting pin 1 of IC3 (a 4081) to ground through an external spst switch does the trick. Any potting compound can be scraped away with the help of a heat gun.
