Jerrold DRZD-3A Converter Only Turn On Modification

by 4x of Group 42

Introduction

This information will turn on the Jerrold DRZD-3A converter for all channels.

Instructions

  • place a jumper between pins 2 and 3 on the terminal strip, this turns on converter. (see diagram)
  • On DRZn and DRZIN models there is a gray wire from board which runs back to the modulator on the right side. Cutting this wire stops the 6dB attenuation. Only the 10dB attinuation descrambleing circuit is left. A SB-3 will work behind the converter now. Some models may have a black wire instead of gray.
