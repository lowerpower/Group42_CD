Jerrold 400 Series Full Service Modification

by 4x of Group 42

Introduction

This information is good for most Non-Addressable Jerrold 400 Series units.

Instructions

  • Remove existing PROM, if any.
  • Short wire-wrap posts 1 and 2 togeather. (#1 has yellow wire on it.)
  • Connect pin 6 of the 4081 IC to pin 10 of the 4011 IC.
