Jerrold 400 DRX-3a Full Service Modification

by 4x of Group 42

Introduction

This information is good for the Jerrold 400 DRX-3A Addressable units.

Instructions

  • Remove existing PROM, if any.
  • Short wire-wrap posts 1 and 2 togeather. (see digram)
  • Cut out transistors on decoder board. (see digram)
  • Install a 100K resistor between pins 6 and 7 of IC U2.
  • Install a 1.8K (1.5k or 2.0K will work in a pinch) between pin 6 of U2 to pin 6 of U4.
  • Install a 10ufd, 25 volt cap between pin 6 of U2 to pin 14 of U3 (+GND U3-14).

AM/FM data receiver

In some cases this additional mod is needed.
