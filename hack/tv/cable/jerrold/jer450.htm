Jerrold 450 Non-Trimode Full Service Modification

by 4x of Group 42

Introduction

This information is good for Jerrold non-trimode 450 series boxes. An external circuit using a 4081 or simalar AND gate, a 2n3906 transitor, two 10K resistors, a 100 ohm resistor, and a SPST switch.

Instructions

  • Logic AND pin 6 of the 4096 and pin 10 of the 4011 using a 4081 or simalar gate.
  • Connect output of AND gate through a 10K resistor to the base of a 2n3906 Transistor.
  • Connect Emitter of 2n3906 to -5 volts (wire post 5) through a 100 ohm resistor.
  • Connect collector to -16 volts (wire post 3) through a 10K resistor.
  • Cut the white wire going from wire post #10 to the metal can area at the metal can area.
  • Connect the white wire to the collector of the 2n3906 transistor through the SPST switch.
  • It might also be neccessary to short together wire posts 1 and 2 to defeat the addressability function.
