Starcom 7 Parental Control Instructions

by 4x of Group 42

Introduction

This information is good for all Starcom 7 and CFT boxes. Some cable companies don't provide this information so they can charge you a monthly fee for the the parental control funcion (they can do these commands remotely.)

Deactivate

You must dectivate parental control before adding or deleting channels to be locked out. The commands are :
  • Press F.
  • Press * or PC.
  • Press 1,2,3,4.
  • Press ENTER.

Choosing Locked Channels

The following steps are required to choose channels to lock out with parental control:
  • Turn on unit and deactivate according to above steps.
  • Tune to the channel to be locked. Note : channels chosen must be 2 digits, i.e. 02,03,..,99.
  • Press * or PC and P indicator will light on disply. (either a small dot or a small p on the display.)
  • Repeat steps 2 and 3 to add more channels.

Activating Parental Control

To activate parental control after choosing channels to lock out select :
  • Press F.
  • Press * or PC.
  • Press Enter.
The unit will now lock out the chosen channels. It will also lock out the channels each time the unit is turned on and off.
