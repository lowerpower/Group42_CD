Starbase Full Service Modification

by 4x of Group 42

Introduction

This information is good for most older Starbase units.

Instructions

  • Remove existing PROM, if any.
  • Insert a 1n914 or 1n4148 diode between pins 7 and 8, cathode (banded) end towards pin 7.
  • Cut the 4 diodes connected to pins 3,4,5 and 10 of the 4015 IC.
Note : if only some of the diodes are cut, some of the channels may not decode.
