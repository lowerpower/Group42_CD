SA8500-321 Turn On

by G-Man of Group 42

The following procedure will allow the 8500-321 converter/descrambler to receive all scrambled and non-scrambled channels, even if it is shut down by the cable company. It is not as nice a fix as the test chip mod and can have unwanted channel switching as a side effect.

Procedure

Locate the main processor IC on the main P.C. board with the part number MN1414 on it. This is IC305 on the circuit board. Now locate jumber W310 just below the IC. Cut this jumper and install a .01uf disk capacitor across the now open jumper. On the lead of the capacitor closest to the IC, solder a 22K resistor from this lead and connect the other end of the resistor to pin 21 on IC305. The descrambler should now properly receive and descramble all channels.

If the descrambler changes channels by itself, the FSK data jumper needs to be cut to turn off the cable companies signal that is interfearing with the above modification.
