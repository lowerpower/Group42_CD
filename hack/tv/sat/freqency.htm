This table gives information about europe's most popular Satellites and the channels on them.


Videocrypt 1 is used only in the U.K.( Sky ). Videocrypt 1 decoder + card required.
Videocrypt 2 is used in the rest of Europe. Videocrypt 2 decoder + card required.
Eurocrypt is Used by various Channels, D2mac decoder+ card required.
Luxcrypt is used by Dutch channels.( RTL 4/5 ,Veronica, SBS 6), Luxcrypt decoder required.
Syster is used in Germany and Spain. Systercrypt decoder + card required
Discret ? .... need info.

Mpeg is used by some channels. No decoder available.

The suggested dish diameter may vary when situated in other countries then Holland.
