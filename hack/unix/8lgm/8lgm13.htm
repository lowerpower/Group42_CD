[8lgm]-Advisory-13.UNIX.SCO-login.15-Apr-1994

PROGRAM:

login(1)

VERSION:

SCO UNIX 3.2v4.2

IMPACT:

Any user on the system can become root.

REPEAT BY:

Exploit details will not be made available until a patch is available.

FIX:

Obtain patch from your vendor.
