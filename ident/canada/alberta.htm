Alberta, Canada

Alberta uses a 2 part license for drivers, a photo ID card that is typical of a US license and a suplimental paper part. ID cards and drivers licenses use the same photo card but have different paper parts as shown below.

INFOS: Part 1 of 2 part license. Card is laminated. Minor's card has birthdate only to identify it. "PROBATIONARY" or "RESTRICTED" may appear to the right of photo.
