Manitoba, Canada

Manitoba uses a 2 part license for drivers, a photo ID card that is typical of a US license and a suplimental paper part. ID cards and drivers licenses use the same photo card but have different paper parts as shown below.

INFOS: Part 1 of 2 part license. Card is laminated. Minor's card has birthdate only to identify it. Not issued for identification only. Also valid "Valid without photo" in red above date of birth or "Valide sans photo" in lower right corner.
INFOS: Part 2 of 2 part license. Must accompany part 1. Paper stock.
