Alberta, Canada

Ontario uses a 2 part license for drivers, a photo ID card that is typical of a US license and a suplimental paper part.

INFOS: Part 1 of 2 part license. Card is laminated. Minor's card has birthdate only to identify it. Minister's signature may appear on Part 1 or Part 2.
INFOS: Part 2 of 2 part license. Paper stock. License may appear with thin black border around information section. Actual size is 7" x 2 3/8".
