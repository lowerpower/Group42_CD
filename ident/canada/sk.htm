Saskatchewan, Canada

Saskatchewan uses a 2 part license for drivers, a photo ID card that is typical of a US license and a suplimental paper part. Also shown is an older paper only license.

INFOS: Part 1 of 2 part license. Card is laminated. Minor's card has birthdate only to identify it.
INFOS: Part 2 of 2 part license. Paper stock. Actual size 3 1/8" x 3 3/4".
INFOS: License on paper stock. Minor's license has birthdate only. Not issued for Identification only. Actual size is 2 1/2" x 3 1/4".
