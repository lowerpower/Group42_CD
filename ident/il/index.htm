INFOS: ID card is laminated. Minor's card has red photo backdrop, "UNDER 21" and "ILLINOIS" printed vertically in red on the sides and a horizontal line in red on the top and bottom. Identification only cards have "ID CARD" printed in white letters in blue box above photo. Also could have "CDL" printed in blue letters in white box above photo. Licenses and IDs issued prior to 4/1/90 had the photo area on the right side. License and ID card may appear with "JIM EDGAR" as Secretary of State.
