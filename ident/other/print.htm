Color Printer Update

By Nickle of Group 42

I've been looking low cost printer capable of reproducing documents, currently in the $400 to $500 range your choice is the HP855, Epson Color Stylus II and the Cannon BJ-610. For photo realistic the HP855 is not an option, although it prints black real well it cannot reproduce colors nearly as well as the Epson or Cannon.

Output of the Epson although good, is not up to the quality of the Cannon. The Cannon would be my choice for photo realistic photos in this class of printer.

There may be other printers out there in this price range by the time you read this, so check before going out and buying one.
