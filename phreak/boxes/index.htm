Box Plans

Boxing - the act of using a box.
Box - A variety of electronic device used to aid in phreaking. The original was the Blue Box, used from the mid 60's to the mid 80's, which allowed long distance phonecalls to be made for free. A variety of other similar instruments accomplishing different tasks have been developed using different box color names, some purely comical, some quite practical.
