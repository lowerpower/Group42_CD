Random Call From Hell

This random phone call really did occur when it did.

It was to my roommate, who was a bit disturbed over the matter, and understandably so.

"Hello [roomies first name]?"
"Yes?" He answered
"Have you registered for selective service? [the US 'draft']"
"um, no."
"(quickly) thank you.". Click.

The next day we had caller ID installed.

It’s a strange world.
