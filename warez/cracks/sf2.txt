Crack for Street Fighter 2 by Capcom

Use Norton Disk Editor to search for
the first occurrence of the following
hex byte sequence in the file SF2.EXE:

73 09 E2 D1

Replace this sequence with:

EB 09 E2 D1

and write your changes to disk.

You can now enter any word at the
password protection screen and it
will be accepted! Happy playing!
