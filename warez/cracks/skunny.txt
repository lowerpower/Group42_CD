Hello,

1. Cheat codes for SKUNNY KART:

 Player 1: Hit F1 key 4 times -->invincible, hit F1 again to stop
           Hit F2 key 2 times to receive a missile

 Player 2: Hit F5 key 4 times -->invincible, hit F5 again to stop
           Hit F6 key 2 times to receive a missile

 Hit F9 to capture a PCX screen --> COPY001.PCX, COPY002.PCX...
