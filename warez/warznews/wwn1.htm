Week In Warez Issue #1 - July 30th, 1995

Welcome to The Week in Warez Newsletter. WWN is a weekly newsletter for the Warez scene! I decided to start WWN because unlike the Music and Demo scene, the Warez scene had no newsletter or disk mag put out on a regular basis. WWN each week will have a warez report that will provide info on every ware released each day. We will also have other cool things like articles, group news, and interviews. This week we have articles from BiSH0P and CYB0RG, plus group news from NTA.

We want you to write in with articles and comments!!

"Internet = Group Wars WHQ?"

by BiSh0P (514)

4 years ago the Warez Scene was kinda peaceful with leading groups like The Humble Guys, Razor and TRSi leading the scene. But then in the past 2 years, this happens! The entire scene is going downhill. Sysops bitchin at each other, groups killing eac h other, lame text files, fake releases, etc.

Then this damn war came onto the net. Damn this was ugly! This may be a strange coincidence but it all started right after I formed the telecomm division of HtC. Nope HtC Crew didn't start the shit, but after that every group was getting a Telecomm Di vision and then the war started.

Channel takeovers, Nick Colliding and Deleted sites are happening everyday day on the net. But why do this? The scene won't do any good that way! I'm not sure if it makes you happy when you takeover a channel, remove operator status to everyone, and r emove ops to yourself so they loose ops. Wow! Big Deal. Thats just a stupid thing to do. Even if the group channel you take over is lame or you don't like someone in the group,it doesn't give you the right to do bad stuff to the scene. Even if the lame g roup does one release per year, they are trying to help the scene their way.

Something good came out of this though, The iRC MAFiA. These guys are semi-professionnal hackers. But unlike other groups they weren't doing shit on irc channel for nothing. They had something to be done the hard way. Beleive it or not i respect these guys. Now i miss the days when CyberCrime International Network was rullin. Most of the good boards were linked together and keeping traders in touch with each other. And File Networks were also good at the time. But hey things change for the good or the bad.

"The Bad Era has Begun."

Messages to traders : Start to get the scene back before it dies!
-BiSh0P [HtC.RAPE.PCY.VDN.TDC.WWN]
